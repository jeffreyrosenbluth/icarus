Icarus

Don't fly too close to the sun.

    How to play

    • Enter players as a comma-separated list in the box below the canvas.
    • Click Play to launch them. Each ball drifts and bounces.
    • If a ball flies too close to the sun, it's eliminated and added to the leaderboard from the bottom up.
    • The last player(s) standing win. The number of survivors is configurable.

    Keyboard

    • ⌘, / Ctrl+, — open preferences
    • ⌘I / Ctrl+I — show these instructions
    • Esc — close any open panel
